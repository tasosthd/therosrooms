The beach

Five kilometres of Blue Flag shoreline

Nea Vrasna's beach has flown the EU Blue Flag for clean water and safe swimming year after year. Shallow, golden, and gentle — the kind of sea where kids run in without a second thought.

By night, the seaside pedestrian promenade comes alive: gift shops, cocktail bars under the stars, and tavernas serving the day's catch. The August moonrise over the bay is the village's open secret.

Getting here

Easy to reach, hard to leave

✈️

From the airport

Thessaloniki Airport (SKG) is a scenic 1h20 drive along the coast — about 96 km. We can arrange a transfer on request.

🏖️

In the village

Supermarket, bakery and the nearest taverna are a 3-minute walk. The whole resort is flat and easy on foot.

⛰️

Worth a day trip

Pine-backed Stavros, the wetlands of the Strymón delta, and the waterfalls near Asprovalta are all close by.
